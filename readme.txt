== Description ==

The AeyTimes Ideas Widget allows people to incorporate their latest AeyTimes ideas into their WordPress blog.

The [AeyTimes Idea Journal and Social Network](http://aeytimes.com/) allows people to [share ideas](http://aeytimes.com/submit/), and [find ideas or inspiration](http://aeytimes.com/search/).

== Installation ==

1. Upload `aeytimes.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in the WordPress administration panel.
1. Drag your widget to the desired position in the 'Widgets' page under 'Appearance'.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Why are my ideas not showing? =

You may have entered your username incorrectly.

= How long does it take for my ideas to appear on the widget? =

It takes at most 15 minutes.
